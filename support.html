Help with Password Wallet for iPhone.

Where is my data stored?

Your passwords, document details, notes, and selected images stay in the App’s private storage on your device. The developer does not operate a cloud vault.

Can the developer recover my vault?

No. The developer cannot access or recover your locally stored vault. Keep your device secure and maintain the device backups you need.

How do I add a password or document?

Choose Accounts or Documents on the home screen, then tap the blue Add button.

How do I edit an entry?

Open a saved card and tap Edit. Your existing values and document images are retained unless you change them.

Why does the App request photo access?

Access is used only when you choose document images or save a copy to Photos. Images are not uploaded to the developer.

What happens if I delete the App?

Deleting the App removes its local data from that device, subject to device backups you control.
